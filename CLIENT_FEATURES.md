# WOTCMaster - Live Platform Features

## Your Complete WOTC Optimization Solution

WOTCMaster is a comprehensive enterprise platform designed to maximize your Work Opportunity Tax Credits through automation, AI-powered intelligence, and seamless integrations.

---

## Core Platform Capabilities

### Three Powerful Portals

**Employee Self-Service Portal**
- Mobile-friendly questionnaire completion
- AI-powered question simplification for any reading level
- Save progress and resume anytime
- Real-time eligibility feedback

**Employer Management Portal**
- Workforce dashboard with KPIs and analytics
- Employee screening oversight and tracking
- Hours worked management with payroll integration
- Credit calculations and projections
- Billing and subscription management

**Administrator Console**
- Platform-wide employer management
- Revenue tracking and analytics
- State automation configuration
- System-wide reporting and exports

- **Configurable Settings**: Notification preferences, branding options, and billing setup

- **Progress Tracking**: Save and resume functionality with completion percentages
- **Mobile-Optimized Design**: Works perfectly on phones, tablets, and desktops

## WOTC Eligibility & Certification
